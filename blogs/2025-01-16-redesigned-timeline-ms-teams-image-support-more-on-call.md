Hey there, firefighters 🧑‍🚒 Here’s what’s shipped for you this week: 👀 Refreshed Timeline to Give You Clear Insights at a Glance The incident timeline has been refreshed for improved scanning and readability, ensuring you can fully track and understand your incident’s progress at a glance. Events are now grouped by date and display more concisely, with expandable accordion sections when you need more detail. Chat messages now show a convenient two-line preview in the collapsed state, with full content easily accessible in the expanded view.

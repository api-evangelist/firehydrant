Hey there, Firefighters 🧑‍🚒 Here’s what’s shipped for you this week: ✨ Auto-Create Incidents from Signals Rules Now you can automatically create incidents from Alert Trigger Rules within Signals! This highly requested feature streamlines your incident response workflow by automatically declaring incidents for critical alerts without requiring manual acknowledgment. To use this feature, head to the Teams tab > click into your Team > choose Alert Triggers.

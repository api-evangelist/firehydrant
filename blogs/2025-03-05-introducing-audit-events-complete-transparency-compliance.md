Hey there, firefighters 🧑‍🚒🚒 Here’s what’s shipped for you this week: ✅ Audit Events Now Available for Enterprise Organizations When something goes wrong, the first question is always: what changed? Today, we're introducing Audit Events — a powerful way to bring enterprise-grade transparency and accountability to your FireHydrant organization. Audit Events provide a clear record of who created, updated, or deleted resources across your FireHydrant Organization.
